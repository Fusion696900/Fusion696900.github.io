This weeks topic

This week's topic will be how to change a tire. It has come to my attention that a lot of people don't know how to change a tire so this week I will break it down for you guys. The first step to changing a tire is identifying the problem a lot of the time you can simply patch the tire. Step 2 after you have decided you need to change the tire you will need to locate the spare tire on your vehicle. Step 3 is to remove the spare tire ang get your jack and wrench. Below I will place a diagram so you can see what I'm talking about. Now that you have the tire, jack, and the wrench the first thing you're going to want to do is make sure your parking brake is on. next you will want to take the lug nuts off of your tire while the car is still on the ground as to make it easier. After this you will want to make sure you have something blocking the tires so that they can not roll just to be safe. Once all prior steps are completed you will want to grab the carjack and place it securely on preferably the frame of your vehicle, but the axel will also work if it needs to but under no circumstances place it so that it is lifting on anything besides them.


The next step is to lift the car with your jack and pull the tire off and place it in your trunk. After that put the spare tire on and closely put the lug nuts on by hand. Final lower the car slowly then use the wrench to hand tighten the lug nuts and then cleanup and drive to your nearest tire shop to replace the tire. And boom just like that you're all done I will attach a video link to show and hopefully explain in greater detail how to change a tire.
